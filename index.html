About the Romans

The Romans were an ancient civilisation that emerged in the 8th century BC (753 BC) and grew into a powerful empire until the western part of the empire collapse in the 5th century AD (476 AD). They originated as a small city-state called Rome in present-day Italy and expanded their territory through military conquests, becoming one of the most influential civilisations in history.
